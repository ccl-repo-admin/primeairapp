# Prime Air — Go-To-Market Strategy

## Target Customer Profile

### Primary ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

**Company:** Independent HVAC company, residential and/or light commercial
**Size:** 4–25 technicians
**Revenue:** $750K – $5M annually
**Location:** Sun Belt states first (TX, FL, AZ, GA, NC, SC, TN) — HVAC volume is highest year-round
**Current tools:** Workyard or paper timesheets + QuickBooks + text message dispatch
**Pain:** Payroll errors, no visibility into where techs are, lost billable time, can't scale

### Buyer Persona

**"Mike the HVAC Owner"**
- Age: 35–55
- Works in the business daily (owns 2–3 trucks, may still run calls himself)
- Makes all software decisions himself (no IT department)
- Evaluates on: cost, ease of setup, will my guys actually use it?
- Current fear: spending $400/mo on software his guys won't adopt
- Spending cap sweet spot: $200–$400/month for a platform that does everything
- Makes buying decisions based on: peer recommendations, Google search, YouTube demos

**"Amanda the Office Manager"**
- Age: 30–50
- Runs dispatch, invoicing, customer calls, and payroll — all herself
- Is the de facto champion for any new software (she'll veto bad tools)
- Values: saves her time, doesn't create new work, easy to explain to techs
- Pain: data entry between systems, chasing unpaid invoices, manually approving timecards

## Phase 2: Soft Launch (Months 1–3)

### Goal: 50 Paying Companies, Learn Quickly

**Channel 1: Direct Outreach to HVAC Facebook Groups**

The HVAC community is extremely active on Facebook. These groups have 50K–400K members of HVAC business owners:
- HVAC Business Owners & Managers (private group, 120K members)
- HVAC Masters of Iron (400K members, owner-operators)
- HVAC Business Tips & Tools

Approach:
- DO NOT spam these groups — banned immediately
- Instead: answer questions about software, payroll, dispatch as an expert
- When someone asks "what GPS tracker do you use?" — answer thoroughly, mention Prime Air naturally
- Post case studies: "We helped this 8-tech company cut payroll time by 3 hours/week" with real data from beta customers
- Run a community Q&A: "I built software for HVAC companies — ask me anything about running your crew"

Target: 15 companies from Facebook outreach in Month 1–3.

**Channel 2: Google Search Ads (Targeted)**

Budget: $2,000/month
Target keywords:
- "HVAC technician time tracking app" ($3–8 CPC, high intent)
- "HVAC dispatch software" ($5–12 CPC)
- "Workyard alternative for HVAC"
- "HVAC work order software"
- "HVAC service scheduling app"

Ad copy angle:
> "The Only App Built for HVAC — GPS Time Tracking + Work Orders + Invoicing. Free 14-Day Trial. No Credit Card."

Landing page: dedicated to each keyword cluster (time tracking page, dispatch page, invoicing page)

Target: 20 trial signups/month at $100 CAC = 20 conversions/month.

**Channel 3: YouTube Demo Videos**

HVAC business owners research software on YouTube.

Video ideas (shoot these before launch):
1. "Prime Air Demo — Full Walkthrough" (10 min) — complete product tour
2. "How to Clock In With GPS — Prime Air Mobile App" (3 min) — for techs
3. "Dispatching Your HVAC Crew With Prime Air" (5 min) — for office managers
4. "Sending an Estimate and Collecting Payment on Site" (4 min) — revenue focus
5. "Prime Air vs. Workyard — What's the Difference?" (6 min) — comparison for high-intent searchers

Optimize titles and descriptions for search: "HVAC dispatch software," "HVAC time tracking app," "HVAC work order software."
