**Pricing FAQs**

*Is there a contract?*
No. Month-to-month on all plans. Cancel anytime.

*What counts as a "technician"?*
Any active field employee. Office-only staff (admin, dispatchers) are not counted.

*Can I try it free?*
Yes. 14-day free trial, no credit card required. Full Pro features during the trial.

*What if I have seasonal fluctuation in techs?*
You can adjust your technician count any month. Add a tech in June, remove in October — you only pay for active users.

*Does Prime Air charge per job or per invoice?*
No per-transaction fees for Prime Air. Stripe charges 2.9% + $0.30 per card transaction (standard Stripe rates). ACH is 0.8%, max $5.
